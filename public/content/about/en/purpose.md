# The Purpose of TCF

## Why TCF exists?
Transsion Core Federation was founded to solve the fragmentation and security issues within Transsion community groups. Before TCF, groups often faced unchecked scamming, phishing, and inconsistent rule enforcement.

## Core Functions
- **Centralized Enforcement**: One ban in one group applies to all, making the community safer.
- **Resource Verification**: Ensuring that shared modules and tools don't contain harmful code.
- **Conflict Resolution**: Providing a formal authority to mediate disputes between group admins and members.
- **Official Hub**: TCF serves as the bridge between developers and the end-users.

**TCF is the guardian of the Transsion community.**
