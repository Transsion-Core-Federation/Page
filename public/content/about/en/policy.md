# TCF Federation Policy

These federation rules are officially enforced across all Infinix, Tecno, and Itel Telegram community groups under the Transsion community. Any violation of these rules will result in sanctions without exception, regardless of group, role, or status.

## Oversight & Coordination
TCF | Transsion Core Federation functions as the core authority responsible for supervision, coordination, and enforcement of rules across all affiliated Telegram community groups. It is the centralized authority for Infinix, Tecno, and Itel Telegram community groups, dedicated to maintaining security, order, and technical standards across the community.

TCF exists to ensure security, order, and compliance, and to protect the community from threats such as phishing, scams, impersonation, malware distribution, and other forms of abuse.

## Violation Protocols
Federation rules focus on maintaining a secure environment. Enforcement covers, but is not limited to:
- Phishing, scam, or fraud
- Impersonation of staff
- Distribution of malware
- Piracy and illegal content
- Flooding or disruptive behavior
- Community harassment

Sanctions include warning, kick, ban, or FBAN, applied federatively across all groups. Violations on one platform impact status across the entire group community.

Decision making for unlisted violations rests on joint coordination between federation and group administrators. These policies may be adjusted over time as new threats emerge.

## Reporting & Integrity
Members are expected to actively maintain community integrity. Threats must be reported to group admins or directly to TCF. **TCF is not optional. These standards protect the entire community.**
