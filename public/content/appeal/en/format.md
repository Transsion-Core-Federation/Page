### Format & Example

To keep things smooth and easy, please use the format below for your appeal in the federation group. Be as honest as possible!

→ Appeal Format:
```
#appeal
Log link: (copy from the log channel)
Clarification: (tell us what happened in your own words)
Agreement: (your commitment to follow the rules going forward)
```

→ Filling Example:
```
#appeal
Log link: https://t.me/TranssionCore2/1
Clarification: I accidentally spammed because my auto-clicker was still on.
Agreement: I promise not to use any automated tools in the group again.
```
