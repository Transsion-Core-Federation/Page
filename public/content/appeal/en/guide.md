### How to Submit an Appeal

Hi there! If you believe your FBAN was a misunderstanding or you'd like to clarify what happened, you're in the right place. We appreciate your honesty—it helps our admin team review your case fairly and accurately.

→ Some Quick Notes:
• Take a moment to check your FBAN reason via the button or in the log channel.
• Outcomes depend on the nature and severity of the violation.
• While we can't approve every appeal, every request is carefully reviewed.
• Please avoid sending multiple appeals. Just send it once and hang tight for our response.

An FBAN doesn't have to be the end. We value growth and responsibility. *Good luck!*
