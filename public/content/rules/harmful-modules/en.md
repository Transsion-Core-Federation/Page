# Distributing Harmful Modules

**Rules**: Members are prohibited from sharing modules that risk damaging devices (such as Bootloop or Brick). Device safety is our collective responsibility.

**Consequences**: Ban or Fban.

**Explanation**: We prioritize our members' device security. Before sharing any module, ensure it has been tested or provide a clear risk warning. Deliberately sharing harmful modules will result in strict disciplinary action.
