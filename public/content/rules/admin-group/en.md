# Group Admin

**Rules**:
- **Reporting to Federation Admin**: Group admins are required to report any rule violations by members to the Federation Admin through official channels.
- **Following Federation Rules**: All managers of affiliated groups must comply with and align their group policies with the primary rules applicable within the federation.

**Consequences**: Warning or removal of Group Admin status.

**Explanation**: Group Admins serve as the primary bridge between local communities and federation policies. Rule synchronization is crucial for maintaining the harmony of the TCF ecosystem.
