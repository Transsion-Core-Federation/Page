# Abuse of Admin Power

**Rules**: Administrators are prohibited from using their authority for personal interests or performing disciplinary actions (such as Fban) against members without clear and objective reasons.

**Consequences**: Kick, Ban, or Fban.

**Explanation**: We uphold justice and fairness. Admin authority is granted to maintain group order, not for personal gain or biased enforcement against members.
