# Doxing and Privacy

**Rules**: Sharing personal data (Doxing) or sensitive information belonging to others without consent is prohibited. Please respect the privacy of fellow group members.

**Consequences**: Kick or Ban/Fban.

**Explanation**: Personal information security is our priority. Actions that violate someone else's privacy will be strictly disciplined to ensure all members feel safe during discussions.
