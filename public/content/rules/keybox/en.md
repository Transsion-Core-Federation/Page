# Keybox

**Rules Regarding Keybox Sharing**:
TCF emphasizes that **keyboxes are free and open-source credentials** intended for personal, non-commercial use. Any attempt to sell, trade, or commercialize keybox access is strictly prohibited and considered a serious violation of community ethics.

**Prohibited Activities**:
- Selling or reselling keyboxes, regardless of any claims of exclusivity or privacy.
- Promoting paid keybox services, vendors, or marketplaces (e.g., keyboxhub, subscription-based offers).
- Misleading others by suggesting that keyboxes are private or worth purchasing.
- Facilitating transactions involving keybox access.

**Community Guidance**:
- Keyboxes are public and free. Never pay to obtain access.
- If you need a keybox, ask in the designated official channels.
- Avoid any parties that charge for keybox access.

**Consequences**: FBAN (Permanent Ban).

**Explanation**: We ensure community resources remain open to all without commercial exploitation.
