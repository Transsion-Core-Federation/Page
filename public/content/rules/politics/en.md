# Politics

**Rules**: Bringing political elements in any form is strictly prohibited within the community environment, including but not limited to:
- Nicknames or usernames.
- Profile pictures, bios, or any other account information.
- Shared media (photos, videos, stickers, or other files).
- Any form of conversation or discussion containing political content.

**Sanctions**: Violations of this rule will result in strict actions, including kick, ban, or fban without prior warning.

**Explanation**: We are committed to maintaining a neutral, comfortable community atmosphere free from conflicts often triggered by political issues. Thank you for your attention and cooperation.
