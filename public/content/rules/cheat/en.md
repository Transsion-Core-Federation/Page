# Cheat

**Rule Adjustments**:
- **Allowed**: The use of **iPad View** in PUBG and Mobile Legends is not considered a cheat as it does not provide an unfair advantage or systematically harm other players.
- **Prohibited**: Any modifications that provide unfair advantages are strictly prohibited, including but not limited to:
  - Maphack
  - Wallhack
  - Damage++
  - Auto-targeting
  - Other detrimental modifications.
- **Prohibition on Discussion & Promotion**: Discussions, sharing, or promotion of prohibited cheats are not allowed in any form across all federation channels, including OOT (*Out of Topic*) groups.

**Sanctions**: Violations of these rules will result in strict actions, including kick, ban, or fban without prior warning.

**Explanation**: We are committed to creating a healthy and fair gaming environment. Sportsmanship is a core value that must be maintained by all community members.

*Last Updated: 13-01-2025*
