# Abuse of Fundraising

**Rules**: It is prohibited to initiate fundraising for projects (such as Custom ROMs) that solely serve personal or private group interests without transparency.

**Consequences**: Ban or Fban.

**Explanation**: Funds collected from members must be used transparently and for the stated purposes. Using community resources for purely personal gain is a violation of our rules.
