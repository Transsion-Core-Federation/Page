# Provocation

**Rules**: Sharing content or making statements intended to incite or provoke (provocation) fellow group members is prohibited.

**Consequences**: Mute, Kick, or Ban/Fban.

**Explanation**: We prioritize a conducive and peaceful group atmosphere. Any form of provocation that could trigger tension or unrest within the group will be addressed strictly.
