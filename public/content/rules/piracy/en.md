# Piracy

**Rules**: All forms of piracy are strictly prohibited within the entire federation environment. We respect the intellectual property rights of developers.

**Consequences**: Kick, Ban, or Fban.

**Explanation**: To maintain a healthy community, here is a clarification regarding our piracy policy:

**Allowed**:
- ReVanced.
- Scene 4 & Scene 5.
- Nekogram and other Telegram mods, provided they do not harm Telegram.

**Prohibited**:
- FKM (Franco Kernel Manager) versions distributed for free illegally.
- Scene mods above version 5 that contain piracy content.
- Any pirated apps, mods, or piracy forms not listed in the allowed list.
- Sharing (content, SS, SR) or discussing piracy in groups, profile channels, or channels where nicknames are visible.

*Last Updated: 19-12-2024*
